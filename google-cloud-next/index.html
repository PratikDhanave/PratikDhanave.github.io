The Presentation

Application Architecture: Monolith to Microservices Migration

Event: Google Cloud Next 2022 — Innovators Hive

Speaker: Pratik Dhanave, Senior Software Engineer at Searce (Google Cloud Premier Partner)

Date: April 2022

This presentation covered the end-to-end journey of decomposing monolithic applications into distributed microservices architectures. Drawing from real production experience across enterprise clients, the talk addressed the technical, organizational, and operational challenges teams face during migration.

Decomposition Strategies

Domain-driven design, bounded contexts, and identifying service boundaries in large monolithic codebases.

Organizational Alignment

Conway's Law in practice — restructuring teams to match the target architecture for sustainable velocity.

Zero-Downtime Deployment

Blue-green deployments, canary releases, and traffic splitting strategies for production migrations.

Production Observability

Distributed tracing, structured logging, and SLI/SLO frameworks for monitoring microservice health.
