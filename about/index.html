Background

I'm a software engineer with 7+ years of experience building cloud-native distributed systems in Go, Python, and TypeScript. My current focus is multi-agent AI — designing frameworks where multiple AI agents collaborate safely under governance constraints in regulated industries.

I've worked across the stack from database internals (PostgreSQL row-level security, BigQuery cost optimization) to cloud infrastructure (GKE, Cloud Run, Kubernetes operators) to application architecture (MARA pattern, agent orchestration, tool-calling pipelines).

Before multi-agent AI, I spent several years at Searce working on Google Cloud consulting engagements, including a Tata Group FinOps engagement that delivered over $12M in BigQuery cost savings.
