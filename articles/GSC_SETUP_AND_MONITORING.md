## What to Monitor (Weekly)

### Performance Tab
- **Queries**: Which keywords people search for to find you
- **Pages**: Which pages get impressions
- **Position**: Current ranking position (1-100)
- **CTR**: Click-through rate from search results

### Example metrics to watch:
```
Keyword: "multi-agent systems"
Position: #14
Impressions: 47 (people saw you in search)
Clicks: 3 (people clicked to visit)
CTR: 6.4% (good target: > 5%)

Action: If CTR < 3%, your meta description needs improvement
Action: If Position = 14-20, you're close to top 10 (create more backlinks)
```

### Coverage Tab
- Shows which pages are indexed
- Look for errors or excluded pages
- Should show: 45+ pages indexed

### Enhancements Tab
- Schema.org markup validation
- Shows if Google understands your BlogPosting markup
- Should show: 19 articles with valid schema

## Monthly Action Items

### Week 1: Monitor Impressions
- Which articles got impressions?
- Which keywords appeared?
- Where's the demand?

### Week 2: Improve CTR
- Update meta descriptions for articles with low CTR
- Current meta descriptions are good, but test variations
- Aim: 5%+ CTR for top articles

### Week 3: Check Rankings
- Which keywords are position 11-30?
- Those are "easy wins" to push into top 10
- Create backlinks specifically for those keywords

### Week 4: Report & Plan
- How many clicks did you get? (target: +20% MoM)
- Which articles gained rankings?
- Where are gaps? (topics with no visibility)

## Pro Tips

1. **Set GSC email alerts** - Get notified of indexing issues
2. **Track competitors** - Use SEMrush/Ahrefs to see who's outranking you
3. **Update old content** - Refreshed articles often re-rank better
4. **Build backlinks strategically** - Focus on keywords where you're close to top 10
