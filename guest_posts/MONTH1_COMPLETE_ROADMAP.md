## Troubleshooting

**"My post has 0 views after 24 hours"**
- DEV.to: Takes 2-4 hours to index. Check again after 4 hours.
- Hashnode: Shares automatically. Check if shared correctly.
- Medium: If direct, promote on Twitter/LinkedIn
- Twitter: Reply to any comments. Engagement = more reach.

**"Medium publication rejected my post"**
- Resubmit to next publication (Towards Data Science, The Startup)
- Or: Publish directly with canonical URL
- Time: 1 hour to resubmit

**"InfoQ is taking forever to respond"**
- Normal. They review thoroughly (1-2 weeks)
- Use that time for Twitter/LinkedIn amplification
- If rejected: submit to other platforms (dev.to, Medium, Hashnode)

**"Companies aren't responding to outreach"**
- Normal. Response rate: 5-10%
- Send 10 emails to get 1 response
- Follow up after 1 week if no response
- Include specific article link (don't be vague)
