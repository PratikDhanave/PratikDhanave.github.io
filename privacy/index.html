Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

This website, pratikdhanave.com, is a personal portfolio and blog operated by Pratik Dhanave. This policy explains what data is collected when you visit this site and how it is used.

The short version: this site uses Google Analytics for basic traffic insights. No personal data is collected beyond what analytics provides. There is no advertising, no user accounts, no forms that collect personal information, and no third-party data sharing.

Google Analytics 4

This site uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with measurement ID G-3BZ8MDPHE1 to understand how visitors use the site. GA4 collects the following types of data:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • General geographic location (country/city level, not precise location)
  • Browser type, operating system, and device category (desktop/mobile/tablet)
  • Screen resolution and language preferences

GA4 uses first-party cookies (prefixed with _ga) to distinguish unique visitors and sessions. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information. Google Analytics processes this data in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

IP anonymization is enabled by default in GA4. Your full IP address is not stored.

Cookies

The only cookies set by this site are those required by Google Analytics 4:

  • _ga — Used to distinguish unique visitors. Expires after 2 years.
  • _ga_<ID> — Used to persist session state. Expires after 2 years.

No other cookies are set by this site. There are no tracking cookies from advertising networks or social media platforms.

No Personal Data Collection

This site does not collect any personal data directly. There are no contact forms, login systems, comment sections, or newsletter sign-up forms that capture personal information. All contact information displayed on the site (email, phone, LinkedIn) is provided voluntarily by the site owner for professional inquiries.

No Advertising

This site does not display any advertisements. No advertising networks or ad-tracking scripts are present on any page.

No Third-Party Data Sharing

Analytics data collected by Google Analytics is not shared with any third party beyond Google's own data processing. No visitor data is sold, rented, or shared with external parties for any purpose.
