Google Cloud Next 2022 — Innovators Hive

Presented "Migrating Monolith Applications into Microservices" at the Google Cloud Next Innovators Hive stage. Full audience, live Q&A, and great community conversations. The presentation covered practical strategies for decomposing tightly coupled monolithic codebases into independently deployable microservices, with specific focus on organisational alignment, service boundary identification using domain-driven design, zero-downtime migration patterns, and production observability with distributed tracing.
